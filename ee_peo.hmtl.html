Department of Electrical Engineering

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

Engineering Ethos

Graduates of Electrical Engineering shall gain the physical, analytical and technical knowledge and skills of Electrical Engineering to meet challenges faced by the Industry and Society.

Diversification

Graduates of Electrical Engineering shall gain multidisciplinary knowledge through projects and industrial assignments, leading to a sustainable competitive edge in Research and Development.

Ethics and Attitudes

Graduates of Electrical Engineering shall have professional ethics and proper attitude to be a good human being besides inculcating self assurance and collaboration skills to guide a team while promoting lifelong learning.


Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

  • The students would be qualified to integrate their knowledge on power, control and signal processing techniques to contribute in the fields like renewable energy systems and emerge as successful entrepreneurs as well, endorsing sustainable eco-friendly development.

  • The students would be endowed with the skills to succeed in national and international level examinations to pursue their career with Academia and R& D Labs across the globe, in the field of engineering or management.
